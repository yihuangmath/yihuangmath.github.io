Teaching Experiences

I am currently (Spring 2022) teaching an undergraduate course entitled "Calculus C2" - it's a foundational first year multivariate calculus class for international students with architecture, humanities, social sciences and economics background. This course is simultaneously taught in the classroom and online (via Zhumu and Rain Classroom), with on-campus tutorial classes as well as online (miro.com) tutorials.

In previous semesters, I taught:

  • Calculus C1, Autumn 2021, Autumn 2020;
  • Calculus C2, Spring 2021;
  • Calculus A2 (Eng), Spring 2020;
  • Riemann Surfaces: a hyperbolic approach (graduate-level YMSC course), Autumn 2019.

I was a departmental tutor in the Mathematics and Statistics department at the University of Melbourne during my PhD days. I've tutored subjects at every level:

  • Calculus 2 (first year),
  • Accelerated Mathematics 1 (first year),
  • Group Theory and Linear Algebra (second year),
  • Real Analysis (second year),
  • Complex Analysis (third year),
  • A few replacement Differential Geometry (Masters level) and Complex Analysis (Masters level) tutorial sessions.
